Thesis

My advisors were Mathieu Jan and Laurent Pautet. The title of my thesis is:

Ordonnancement temps réel multiprocesseur pour la réduction
de la consommation énergétique des systèmes embarqués

And a possible English tranlation:

Energy-aware real-time scheduling of multiprocessor embedded systems

Download

The thesis is written in French and can be found here. The slides I used for the defense can also be downloaded here.

It was written using LaTeX and the full sources can be downloaded here. To build it under Debian or Ubuntu, you need to install most of the texlive-* packages and at least the two following Haskell packages: libghc-cairo-dev and libghc-pango-dev.
